Product Categories

Diversity in Products

Pure water for every need and budget - choose from our wide range of RO systems and dispensers.

Domestic RO Systems

Home RO water systems have 8-15 LPH capacity and use sediment filters, carbon filters, and RO membranes to purify water.

Commercial RO Systems

Commercial RO systems have higher capacity (25-500 LPH) for use in hotels, restaurants, and offices.

UV Water Purifiers

UV water systems use radiation to kill microorganisms and are used with carbon filters or RO membranes for complete purification.

Gravity Water Purifiers

Low-cost, simple water purifiers use sediment and activated carbon filters for impurity removal. No electricity needed, ideal for rural areas.

Water Dispensers

Water dispensers provide hot and cold water, used in offices, gyms, and public places. They come in different styles and can be plumbed or refillable.
